About Us

Welcome to UrbanEats Kitchen - Your culinary destination in Birmingham

UrbanEats Kitchen is conveniently located at 140 Bath Row, right in the heart of Birmingham’s vibrant city centre. Our spot is easily accessible by foot, public transport, or car, making it the perfect place to stop by whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or settling in for a relaxed meal. The surrounding area is lively yet welcoming, offering a blend of city energy and local charm that adds to the overall dining experience.

UrbanEats Kitchen is proudly family-run, with roots tracing back to South Asia, and that heritage shines through in the flavours we create. While our menu features a wide range of modern dishes, we blend in subtle South Asian influences—from handcrafted spice mixes to traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. This unique fusion adds warmth, depth, and authenticity to our food, giving every dish a personal touch that reflects our family’s culture and passion for great cooking.
